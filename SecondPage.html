About Us

We are Health AInsights.

Our Mission Statement

Equity Through Innovation.

These are the words that have guided the founding of our startup, HealthAInsights. We believe that healthcare is a right that everyone deserves no matter who they are and where they come from. To ensure equitable access to medical resources and equipment, our startup harnesses Artificial Intelligence on data to deliver valuable insights. With a special focus on low-income and hard-hit communities, our aim is to ensure effective resource allocation and supply chain management by manufacturing companies.

In the face of any health crisis, HealthAInsights will stand with humanity.

What We Do

We help minimize risk and build resilience of supply chains while saving lives in the process. Research from Bain and Company shows 20%-60% in forecasting accuracy with advanced analytics, such as Artificial Intelligence. AI provides unimaginable potential- we're simply the ones harnessing its power to improve the world.
